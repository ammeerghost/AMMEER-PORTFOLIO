About Me

Hi! I'm Ameer Yusuf, a 2D cartoon animator and aspiring digital artist from Katsina, Nigeria. I am passionate about creating compelling stories through animation and design. My work focuses on character design, keyframe animation, and environment sketching, and I am actively building my portfolio with personal projects.

I’m proficient with tools like After Effects, Cartoon animator4, and Photoshop, and I’m constantly exploring new techniques to improve my skills. Currently, I share my work on platforms like Instagram and Newgrounds, where I connect with a vibrant community of artists.

When I’m not animating, I enjoy learning new design trends, experimenting with different art styles, and imagining what the future of animation holds!

I started learning animation through YouTube tutorials and online courses, which has allowed me to develop a strong foundation in storytelling, keyframe animations, and character movement. My current goal is to transition into professional 2D animation projects and contribute to innovative animated series.
